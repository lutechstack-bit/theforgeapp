T Tally · the Forge Filmmaking · Premise Submission
Opening the premise form in Tally…
The Forge app redirects the student to the real Tally form (tally.so/r/mRE0p9). When the student submits there, only the event of submission comes back — the answers stay in Tally.
What actually happens when they submit:
1. Tally collects the answers (we don't see them in the app)
2. Tally fires a webhook with formId, responseId, and any hidden fields we passed in (like student_id)
3. Our app records: "student X submitted form Y at time Z" — nothing more
POST /functions/v1/tally-webhook
{ formId: "mRE0p9", responseId: "r_...",
  hiddenFields: { student_id: "..." } }
